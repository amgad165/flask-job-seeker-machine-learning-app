Title: General Assistant
Webindex: 68507605
Company: Castlebeck
Description: In this role you will be responsible for the general maintenance of the unit and be accountable for the maintenance and safe use of equipment. You will ensure a clean and safe environment is maintained paying particular attention to Health and Safety legislation and environmental health needs. You will carry out weekly health and safety checks within the unit, such as fire alarms, water temperature and central heating. You will also be required to complete minor redecoration and act as a driver of company vehicles as and when required. The successful applicant will have previous experience of working within a similar role and will have a full clean driving licence.
