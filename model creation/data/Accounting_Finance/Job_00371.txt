Title: Treasury Manager

Company: Communicate
Description: A brilliant opportunity has arisen within this great organisation for a Treasury Finance Manager to join a growing treasury team. Reporting in to the Deputy Treasurer the main focus of the role is ensuring that financing plans are in place for each business. The main accountabilities of the role are as follows. All areas around the development of treasury finance plans, including affiliate cash and borrowing plans, dividend plans and developing the annual financing plan for Board of Directors review. Responsible for managing treasury risk management policies and counterparty risk limits with all third party financial institutions. To be eligible for this role you must have experience of working in a similar role in a large corporate environment. An accountancy qualification is preferred, and strong systems skills are essential.
