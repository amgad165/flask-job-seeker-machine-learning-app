Title: Claims Inspector  Flooring
Webindex: 69930907
Company: Eden Brown
Description: A Claims Inspector is required for this company providing a seamless and complete validation/restoration and replacement service for the insurance industry. This is a field position  based from home and covering a geographical area that is set by the head office covering regions in the surrounding post codes of your locality. The work consists of assessing damage to floorcoverings (carpets, woods, ceramics etc), estimating and planning the areas and costing the products for replacement. Retail or industry experience is essential (you may for example come from a carpet sales/fitting background). Training will be given on procedures, report writing etc. Eden Brown is committed to equality in the workplace and is an equal opportunity employer. Eden Brown Ltd is acting as an Employment Agency in relation to this vacancy
