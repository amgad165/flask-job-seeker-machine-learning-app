Title: Mechanical Maintenance Fitter

Description: A fantastic opportunity for a Mechanical Maintenance Fitter has arisen within a growing manufacturing organisation within Stoke on Trent. As a Mechanical Maintenance Fitter you will be working within a small team and be responsible for maintaining all heavy plant machinery within the factory. Duties will involve reactive maintenance  repairing all heavy manufacturing equipment, for example, pump repairs and motor repairs. PPM schedules also need to be adhered on a weekly / monthly basis. Electrical knowledge is also beneficial to complete tasks such as fault finding and minor installation works. A City and Guilds qualification / ONC in Engineering or equivalent is essential as well as a good knowledge of hydraulics and pneumatics.
